FAQ's

 1. **What is WE Air Postpaid service?**

 * It’s the same WE Air packages currently available but with a different payment method, postpaid billing instead of prepaid. With this Plan, you enjoy uninterrupted internet service without the need for recharging.
 * A monthly bill is issued on a fixed date, and you can pay it through any WE store or via the My WE App. This ensures greater service continuity without interruptions.
 * You can also subscribe to additional add-on bundles if you consume your main package during the month. The add-on charges will simply be added to your next bill.
 * Add-ons are valid for a one-time use and do not auto-renew.
 * Any remaining megabytes from your main package will roll over to the next month and will be consumed after the new package is used.
 

 2. **What devices are available with WE Air Postpaid?**

WE Air Postpaid provides high-performance router options either 4G or 5G:

 7. **How do WE Air Postpaid packages work?**

 * The main WE Air Postpaid bundles (250, 400, 600, 900, 1100, and 1400) will be renewed by the bill cycle.
 * Add-ons are available anytime when needed, with no limit on how many you can subscribe to.
 

 8. **What documents are needed to subscribe to WE Air Postpaid?**

 * All you need is your National ID (for Egyptians) and ATP (Ability to Pay) or commitment form according to your bundle.
 * The same conditions as purchasing a regular WE postpaid line apply.
 
**How is the service location determined?**

You can set your registered location through:

 * [Click Here](https://te.eg/wps/portal/te/Personal/We-Air-4G/Check-Availability)
 * Visiting any WE store
 * Call 111 for assistance
 

 9. **Can I use my WE Air router in a different location?**

 * Yes, For We Air higher bundles starting from We Air 600 till We Air 1400, can use the router in the two registered location. But We Air 250 and We Air 400 can only use the router in one location and if you want to add additional concurrent home zone, you will pay 40EGP as an annual fee.
 
**How do I renew my package?**

 * Your main package renews automatically every month if your postpaid bill is settled and there is no early renew as according to your bill cycle.
 
**What happens if I consume all my MBs?**

 * You have flexible options via the My WE app like switching to a different package or subscribing to any available add-on
 
**What is the validity of add-ons?**

 * Each add-on is valid for 30 days from the subscription date and does not renew automatically.
 
**How can I track my consumption?**

 * You can easily monitor your usage through:
 * The My WE mobile app
 * Calling 111 from any WE line or 0155 5000111 from any other network
 
**How do I manage my WE Air Postpaid account through the My WE app?**

 * Download the My WE app from your app store
 * Register using your data SIM mobile number
 * Retrieve the activation code from the router homepage or via SMS if the SIM is used in a phone
 * After activation, you can control your package through MY WE app, the router's home page, or by dialing *979 # when using the sim in a mobile device.
 
**What happens if I leave a 5G coverage area?**

 * Your device will automatically switch to the best available network, such as 4G or 3G, to keep you connected without interruption.
