#### Business Air Terms & Conditions:

 1. WE Business Air Router works on WE Network coverage and is locked on the WE data SIM. 
 2. To subscribe contact your account manager or call 522 for more details
 3. Available routers are (LTE CAT4 router EGP1700 or LTE CAT7 router EGP 2500)
 4. WE Business Air 250 and 400 bundles are used in one location
 5. WE Business Air 600 , 900 & 1100 bundles can be used in two locations
 6. You can add extra location with 40 EGP paid once life time
 7. Bundle automatically renews every 30 days.
 8. Add-on packages are available on-demand and not automatically renewed.

Addon Bundles | Price/EGP | Quota

 * Add-on packages are valid for 30 days
 * Prices Excluding Taxes
 * Change of location requests should be done through customer care 522
 * You can access the router’s dashboard through [My WE App](http://www.te.eg/app) or by accessing the router’s main page using the following steps: 
 * Connect your mobile or computer to the router’s Wi-Fi network, Please refer to the label on the bottom of the router for the default SSID (network name) and Wi-Fi Key (password) . (SSID & Wi-Fi Key is unique for each device).
 * Open your browser and write down on address bar router default IP address: 192.168.8.1 (Default IP wrote on the label on the bottom of the router).
 * Enter default username: admin and default password: admin of Router login page (Default username and password wrote on the label on the bottom of the router)
