FAQ's

 1. **What is WE Air service?**

 * WE Air is a 4G or 5G mobile Router available to work anywhere in Egypt. It works with WE Data SIM card instead of fixed line connectivity.
 * WE Air is locked on WE network and data sim only
 * It can be used in 1 place at a time. Changing zone is available through WE Call center. Or you can pay EGP 40 one time to add new home zone.
 

 2. **What are the specifications of the device?**

WE is offering two devices as follows:

 
**LTE CAT4 Router**
 * High Speed Download 
150Mbps/Upload 50Mbps
 * Wi-Fi Bands: 802.11 b/g/n, 2.4GHz 2*2
 * Supports Up to 32 Wi-Fi Devices
**LTE CAT6 Router**
 * High Speed Download 
300Mbps/Upload 50Mbps
 * Wi-Fi Bands:802.11 b/g/n/ac, 2.4GHz 2x2 & 5GHz 2x2 MIMO
 * Supports Up to 64 Wi-Fi Devices
 

 3. **LTE CAT7 Router**
 * •High Speed Download 
300Mbps/Upload 100Mbps
 * Wi-Fi Bands:802.11 b/g/n/ac, 2.4GHz 2x2 & 5GHz 2x2
 * Supports Up to 64 Wi-Fi Devices
 4. **Do the packages renew automatically or on-demand?**

 * Main packages (250, 400, 600, 900, 1100) renew every 30 days automatically if you have sufficient balance
 * You can subscribe to any of the Add-ons when needed (there is no maximum limit to subscribe to add-ons
 

 5. **What are the required documents to purchase the device/data line?**

 * The same documents you need to purchase any device or data line from WE. National ID for Egyptians
 
**How can the location of the service be determined?**

 * Choose the desired location by[Clicking Here](https://te.eg/wps/portal/te/Personal/We-Air-4G/Check-Availability)
 * Visit one of our branches
 * Call 111 for support
 

 6. **Can I install the device in another location than the one I registered with?**

 * No, it has to be used in the locations already registered on the system. In case installed in another location, the service will not be working.
 
**What is the consumption rules?**

 * 1MB for every 1MB you use on all website all the day times.
 
**How to renew the package?**

 * The main package is renewed every month if there is enough balance.
 * You can also renew your package early through MyWe App and remaining megabytes will be carried over.
 
**What happens when the package is fully consumed?**

 * You can choose any of the following through My WE mobile app
 * Early-renew the package
 * Change to another package
 * Subscribe to any add-on
 
**Will the remaining MBs be carried over to the next month?**

 * Carry-over is available for the main packages only upon on-time renewal
 
**What is the validity of the Add-ons?**

 * The add-on is valid for 30 days from the subscription date and it’s not automatically renewable
 
**How can I know all my consumption details?**

 * You can control your package through MY WE app, the router's home page, or by dialing *979 # when using the sim in a mobile device
 * Call 111 from any WE number or 0155 5000111 from any other number.
 
**How can I use MY WE app to manage my package?**

 * Download the My WE mobile app from the app store
 * Register with mobile number of the data sim
 * The activation code can be received on the router's main page or on your mobile device
 * After activation, you can manage your account (charging, renewing packages, add-ons, consumption ... etc.)
