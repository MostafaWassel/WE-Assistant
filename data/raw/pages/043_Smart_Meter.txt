#### Smart Meters

Smart meters is an electronic device gives both you and your energy provider accurate and regular updates on how much electricity or gas you use.

Smart meters go the extra step in helping you keep track of how much utilities your household is actually using.

Unlike an analogue meter, a smart meter records your usage of utilities at half-hourly intervals, and sends the data daily to your retailer using 4G technology.

#### Why should you get a smart meter?

###### Automatic Readings: No Requirement to Submit Readings Minimizing Site Visit

###### Accurate invoices: no more estimated bills

###### Enables you to accurately track energy consumptions using regular actual reads

###### Great control over energy consumption

###### Protect the environment by reducing Co2 emissions.
